About Me

Born and raised in Arizona, I moved to Colorado at 18, relocating to Denver a year later. Working for the dental industry, coordinating dental insurance for a local office for two years. I decided that is not what I wanted to do with the rest of my life, so I looked into programs to give me valuable and applicable skills that I could use in the work force. I've always been a problem solver and have always enjoyed that aspect of the coding world. Now, working full time, I am an aspiring Web Developer.

Though I am dedicated to my school and furthing myself and my career I am not just a coder. I am a proud mother of two...cats. Mia (shamelessly named after Mia Thermopolis from 'The Princess Diaries') and Berlioz (AKA Little B...because sometimes he is a little b). I am also a huge book nerd and musical geek. When I have the time, I've been known to hole myself up and read as many as 8 books a week. I know...maybe a little much, but it's what I do. I studied musical theatre in school. That has been my main passion for as long as I can remember, and that continues to be true even today.

Although I love nerding out on my books or musicals, I also am very into athletics. Don't hate me Denver, but I am loyal DiamondBacks fan...not the Rockies. As much of a spectator I am I also enjoy playing the sport with friends. I am a person of very vast hobbies and intrests and look forward to what my future holds.
